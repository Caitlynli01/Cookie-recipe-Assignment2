Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chef: Caitlyn Li

Finished Cookies

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Butter (softened) 1 cup
White sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Eggs 2
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9–11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Let cookies cool before serving.

Best Practices & Tips

  • Use room-temperature butter for smoother mixing.
  • Chill dough for 30 minutes for thicker cookies.
  • Don’t overbake — cookies firm up as they cool.
  • Add a pinch of sea salt on top for extra flavor.
  • Store cookies in an airtight container.
